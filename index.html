7 Ways To Save Money

  • Do Not Buy coffee every morning: Buying coffee everyday can be expesive. Let's say a medium coffe cost $4.50 and you you buy that coffee everyday so $4.50*30(days)= $135, you are basically wasting $135 per month. Insead of buying coffee from starbucks or local coffee store, make coffee at your dorm or you house, it is so easy and you can save so much money."
  • Do Not buy brand new Textbook: Many Textbooks are expesive. Also do not try to buy the book from the university bokstore, try to see if you can rent it from Chegg or Amazon.
  • Use everything the Univesity is offering: Take full advantage of the things usniversity is offering for example, most university have free gym membership for the students, use that to your advantage. There are other services like writing center, tutoring center, take a full advantage of the services.
  • Limit the times you eat outside: Try to take advantages of the events that the university host where they have free food. if you have bought meal plan use it carefully
  • Have roommates: If you ae living outside of campus try to have roommates. it will save you money by splitting the the rent.
  • Do not get a car: Getting a car is expensive, you have to pay for gas, pay for the insurance, also pay have for the oil changes, also getting a parking pass from you university is expesive, instead you should use public transportation
  • DO not be careless with credit cards If you do get a credit card, choose the one that has lowest interest rate and only charge what you can afford to pay off in full each month. This will help you avoid late fees and costly credit card debt.
